I was introduced to programming many years ago via a comp sci class in highschool. Back then, we used BASIC, which, back then, was just as exciting as playing Atari. I love to be creative and artsy and see my finished pieces out in the world. It's my way of leaving my mark on the planet. There's definitely an infinite amount of knowledge out there to learn, and learning new things is a big part of my daily ritual. And, with all those things, there are an infinite number of new, creative endevors to find, explore and give to the world.
